Maximizing Individual Potential for Greater Self-Sufficiency

Mission

To provide person-centered services that enhance the quality of life for people with disabilities in the areas of employment, life skills, and community inclusion.

Vision

ProAct will be the provider of choice for person-centered services that enhance the quality of life for people with disabilities. With a purpose to maximize individual potential for greater self-sufficiency.
